Description:

The Canal & River Trust is working with Hackney Council and Transport for London to address fly-tipping and unauthorised uses under the A12 East Cross Route where it crosses the Lee Navigation.

To enable site clearance works and long-term site improvements to be undertaken, moorings will be suspended under the East Cross Route Bridge until 31 March 2019. 

Location:
Lee Navigation
Starts At:
Bridge 14, Eastway Road (A106)

Bridge 14A, Newham Way
